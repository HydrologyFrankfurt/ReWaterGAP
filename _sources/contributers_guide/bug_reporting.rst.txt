.. _bug_reporting:

###############
Reporting a bug
###############

To improve ReWaterGAP's stability and functionality, we rely on user-submitted bug reports and enhancement requests. These are tracked through Github's `issue system <https://github.com/HydrologyFrankfurt/ReWaterGAP/issues/new/choose>`_. When submitting a report or request, please choose the appropriate category and complete the issue form thoroughly. This helps both the core development team and the community understand the scope of the issue and address it effectively.
